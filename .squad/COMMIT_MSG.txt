fix(backend): replace immutable cache-control with no-cache on model endpoint

Cache-Control: immutable on /api/model/v1/generator.onnx caused browsers
to permanently serve the pre-retrain (blank-producing) model from cache,
ignoring the new generator.onnx committed at c339b72.

Changed to no-cache so the browser always revalidates via ETag:
- 304 Not Modified (fast) when model is unchanged
- 200 with new model when retrained
