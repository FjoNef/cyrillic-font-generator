## Technical Notes

**Root cause:** `quantize_dynamic` calls `replace_gemm_with_matmul()` internally, transposes weight initializers in-place, but leaves `value_info` shape annotations stale.

**Solution:** Strip those stale entries before calling `quantize_dynamic`, allowing `infer_shapes_path` to recompute from scratch.

**Size target:** Met. ~16 MB estimated (brotli) < 20 MB target.
