### Key Discovery

Root cause: `quantize_dynamic` internally calls `replace_gemm_with_matmul()`, which transposes Gemm weights in-place but does NOT update corresponding `value_info` shape annotations. Stripping these redundant entries before quantization allows the quantizer to recompute shapes fresh after its transformation.

### Implementation
